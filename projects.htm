Welcome to my projects area. This space represents a wide breadth of software design I have encountered. All of these projects share a commonality: specializing for a solution and then optimizing after the fact. A phrase I have always appreciated regarding software design is, "can we do better." This remark is applicable to many aspects of life and academia, but becomes pertinent in the world of software due to the flexibility in solving problems. After experiencing how a system should behave, then heeding analytical complexity, a solution can be tailored to the unique tones of a large scale problem. These projects represent some of my attempts to integrate myself into a set of problems that require a deep dive into software development in order to make adequate solutions.
